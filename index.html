Transport Plan

Ek jagah se doosri jagah kaise jana hai — har option ki detail

🚐
Tempo Traveller (12-seater) BEST OPTION ⭐
Kochi se book karo — full trip ke liye
10 logon ke liye yeh sab se acha option hai. Ek gaadi, ek driver, apni timing. Koi dependency nahi. Driver raat ko apni jagah soyega, subah ready rahega.

Cost: ₹4000–5000/day. 8 days = ₹32,000–40,000 total = ₹3200–4000/person poore trip ka transport.

Kahan se book karo: Kochi mein local travel agents, MakeMyTrip/Goibibo pe "Tempo Traveller Kochi" search karo, ya hotel se arrange karwa lo.
✅ Apni timing — koi bus/train wait nahi
✅ 10 logon ke saath sab ek saath
✅ Ghaat roads pe comfortable
✅ Luggage problem nahi
❌ Ek hi point se start-end
❌ Advance booking chahiye (at least 1 month pehle)
🛵
Bike Rental (within cities)
Munnar, Varkala mein local explore ke liye
Agar Tempo Traveller hai toh generally zaroorat nahi. But Varkala cliff pe ya Munnar town mein locally ghoomne ke liye Activa rent karna fun hota hai.

Cost: ₹400–600/day per bike. 5 bikes = ₹2000–3000/day.

Kab use karo: Day 7 (Varkala full day) ya Day 3 (Munnar local sightseeing).
✅ Local freedom
✅ Scenic ride mein alag feel
❌ Group split ho jaata hai
❌ Ghaat roads pe experience chahiye
📱
Ola/Uber/Rapido
Emergency ya short distances ke liye
Kerala mein Ola available hai but Kochi ke bahar availability limited hai. Thekkady, Munnar mein surge pricing aa sakti hai. Main transport ke liye rely mat karo.

Use karo: Kochi mein pehle din Fort Kochi ghoomne ke liye ya Trivandrum station pe last day.
✅ Kochi mein accha kaam karta hai
❌ Remote areas mein unavailable
❌ 10 logon ke liye multiple cabs expensive
🚌
KSRTC (Kerala State Bus)
Budget option — recommended nahi
Cheapest option hai (₹150–300/person per route) but 10 logon ke saath coordination bahut mushkil. Timing flexible nahi. Luggage problem. Ghaat routes pe time zyada lagta hai.

Use karo: Agar emergency mein Tempo breakdown ho jaye toh backup.
✅ Sab se sasta
❌ Group ke liye impractical
❌ Fixed timing, multiple changes

Veg Food Guide 🌿

Veg logon ke liye kya kya milega — har jagah ki detail

✅ Kerala mein Veg logon ke liye overall good news

Kerala actually ek achi jagah hai veg logon ke liye — South Indian food mostly veg-friendly hota hai. Idli, dosa, appam, puttu, avial, sambar, rasam, Kerala thali — sab veg mein excellent milta hai. Seedha bolo "veg only" — log samajhte hain.

🍌 Kerala Sadya — MUST TRY (Fully Veg)

Traditional Kerala feast — banana leaf pe 20+ dishes. Poori tarah veg hoti hai by tradition. Rice, sambhar, rasam, avial, thoran, pachadi, payasam, papad — sab ek saath. ₹250–350/person. Fort Kochi ya Alleppey mein karo. Trip ka best food memory guaranteed.

🚢 Houseboat — Veg Meals

Booking ke time clearly bolna — "Pure vegetarian meals required". Most operators ₹250–500 extra per head lete hain for custom veg menu. Jain food bhi arrange hoti hai if needed. Avial, vegetable curry, sambar rice, appam — fresh cooked onboard.

🍃 Thekkady + Munnar — Veg Options

Hill station areas mein tourist-friendly restaurants hain. South Indian thali, chapati + dal, veg kurma, aloo paratha — sab milta hai. Spice plantation mein cardamom chai + veg snacks milte hain.

🏖️ Varkala Cliff — Best Veg Cafe Scene

Varkala mein international-style cafes hain jahan excellent veg food milta hai. Hummus, falafel, veg wraps, smoothie bowls, banana pancakes, Kerala thali. North Cliff area pe sab best milta hai. Prices slightly higher (₹200–400/dish) but quality good.

🚢 Kochi Cruise — Veg Food

Sunset cruise mein buffet hota hai. Booking ke time "veg preference" bolo — operators usually accommodate karte hain. South Indian veg items + snacks included in the package.

💡 Pro Tips for Veg Group
1. Har restaurant mein alag se order karo — "veg thali" usually available hai everywhere
2. Houseboat + cruise booking ke time advance mein veg requirement clearly mention karo in writing/WhatsApp
3. Coconut-based Kerala curries (avial, olan, kootu) — sab veg hain aur absolutely delicious
4. Appam + vegetable stew — quintessential Kerala veg breakfast, must try
5. Street food: Banana chips, achappam, halwa — sab veg hain
